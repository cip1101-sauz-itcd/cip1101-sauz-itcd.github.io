HOW DO I RETURN MY ORDER?

You will have 2 return options:

  1. Return via Postal Service (postage by consumers)
  2. Return in one of our own Retail Store (free returns)

Please kindly follow the following steps:

  1. Contact our Customer Service team here
  2. Please provide your order number and cause for return to our Customer Service staff.
  3. Print the return form(s) that our Customer Service team will provide you.
  4. Simply place the item to be returned in a box, attach your return form, and close the box.
  5. Return your returns to your selected courier service.
  6. Take note of the tracking number stated on the shipping label provided by the postal service as reference.

In-Store Returns (free returns)

FAQs

Does my card need international purchases enabled?
Yes, we recommend contacting your bank to have your card enabled for foreign purchases. If foreign purchases are required, you will be informed throughout the checkout process.

Please be aware that foreign orders may be subject to a minor transaction fee imposed by some banks.

Can I pay for my order with multiple methods?
No, payment for The Shoe Rack orders can't be split between multiple payment methods.

Can I pay for my order with cash?
Yes, cash on delivery is available for eligible orders.

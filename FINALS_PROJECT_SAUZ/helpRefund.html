WHEN CAN I EXPECT MY REFUND?

Your refund will be completed once our quality assurance team has evaluated the goods you've returned and determined that it complies with our return policy. This quality check can take up to two weeks, although it usually takes much less.

The refunds from The Shoe Rack Store to your payment services provider takes 1-2 days once the return has been processed (for example your credit card supplier). Please keep in mind that the time it takes a payment services provider to process your refund varies depending on the supplier. If you paid by Cash on Delivery, refunds will usually take longer to complete.

If you paid by Cash on Delivery, our customer care representatives will send you a secure refund form with your bank information. You minimize any extra delays caused by inaccurate information, be sure to give complete and precise bank account information (exact account name, bank name, account number). We might not be able to perform a straight refund if you do not have a bank account. Instead, you will receive a voucher for the amount of the refund towards your next online purchase, subject to terms and limitations.

For further information and especially if you do not receive your refund within 21 business days, please feel free to call our customer care hotline: (02)-570-60-32
